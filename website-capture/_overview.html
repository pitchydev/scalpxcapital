The website at a glance

ScalpX is a single-page experience that turns a crypto trading community into a premium product story. It opens on a 3D chrome-coin hero, then guides visitors through the problem, the platform, the proof and the call to action - each section animated as it enters the viewport, while staying fast on phones.

Built with

  • Next.js (App Router) + React 19
  • React Three Fiber, drei & postprocessing
  • Three.js - chrome coins, HDRI, bloom
  • Framer Motion - scroll & reveals
  • Tailwind CSS v4 design tokens
  • Lenis smooth scrolling

Experience highlights

  • Scroll-driven 3D hero with camera dolly
  • Pinned, cross-fading "how it works" flow
  • Count-up stats & tilt-in feature cards
  • Auto-scrolling testimonial marquee
  • Springy education chips & glowing CTA

Engineered for speed

  • WebGL canvas pauses when off-screen
  • content-visibility on heavy sections
  • IntersectionObserver-gated animations
  • Shared geometries & capped DPR
  • Reduced-motion fallbacks throughout

How it works

A guided, scroll-pinned flow

A pinned, MetaMask-style sequence walks through the journey step by step, cross-fading panels and visuals as the user scrolls.
  • Imperative scroll engine (rAF) for buttery transitions
  • Ghost step numbers, staggered bullets, progress rail
  • No per-frame React re-renders - smooth on every device
